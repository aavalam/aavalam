Shoes moves to the 2nd Generation

August 23 2009, 2:41 AM  by Victor Goff
On the Shoes ML, _why had implied that he'd take a break.

- it is even harder during the summer. (25 May 2009)

- A Cup Full Of Why: 32 Why The Lucky Stiff Links

- Decentralization of _why's Projects
