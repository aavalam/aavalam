Video on Green Shoes

October 3 2011, 5:05 AM  by ashbb

Luckily I found a solution to make Ruby/GStreamer work well on Windows!

- Just only download and install: GStreamer-WinBuilds-GPL-x86.msi
The latest Green Shoes for Windows can run the following snippet.
Look at a tiny demo.

require 'green_shoes'
Shoes.app do
  v = video 'http://www.rin-shun.com/shoes/AdventureTimewithFinnandJakeFinnTime.mp4'
  button('play'){v.play}
  button('pause'){v.pause}
  button('stop'){v.stop}
  button('+5 sec') {v.time += 5000}
end

I know there are still some kinks to be worked out.
But today must be my lucky day. :)
