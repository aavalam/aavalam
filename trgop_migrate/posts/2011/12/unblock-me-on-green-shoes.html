Unblock Me on Green Shoes

December 2 2011, 7:42 AM  by ashbb

Unblock Me is a very famous puzzle game. It was featured in the Apple Store's "What's Hot" list and "Highly Addictive Games" list.

The goal is to get the red block out of the board by sliding the other blocks out of the way.

I wrote two small codes, i.e. unblockme.rb and make_game.rb.

You can use make_game.rb to make puzzles by yourself. So, you can play lots of puzzles in the Internet and also create your original puzzles. ;-)

Watch this demo video and play Unblockk Me on Green Shoes!

Have fun,
