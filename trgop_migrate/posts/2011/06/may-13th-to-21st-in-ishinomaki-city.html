May 13th to 21st in Ishinomaki City

June 1 2011, 7:32 AM  by ashbb

I went to Ishinomaki City, Miyagi Prefecture, one of the worst Tsunami affected region in the 2011 great east Japan earthquake, and joined a volunteer activity operated by Peace Boat (NGO).

On the night of May 13th, participants gathered at Shinjuku Central Park and went to the campus of Senshu University in Ishinomaki by bus.

In early the next morning, set up a tent in designated area and had a brief meeting to take over the volunteer activities. Then, for the next eight days, carried out volunteer activities called CLEAN, DELIVERY, KITCHEN and STORE.

- CLEAN    : clean-up operation
- DELIVERY : distribution of hot meals and relief goods
- KITCHEN  : cook hot meals
- STORE    : operation of storage areas where relief goods donated are kept
