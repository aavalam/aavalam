simple-sqlite3.rb works with Green Shoes

April 9 2011, 8:09 AM  by ashbb

I could run the Red Shoes' simple-sqlite3.rb with Green Shoes on my Windows 7 laptop!

Green Shoes doesn't include sqlite3 library. So, need a little bit preparation.

- download sqlite3-1.3.3-x86-mswin32-60.gem
- gem install sqlite3-1.3.3-x86-mswin32-60.gem
- download sqlite3.dll and bring it under c:\ruby\bin

That's it!

Now you can run this snippet with Green Shoes. ;-)
