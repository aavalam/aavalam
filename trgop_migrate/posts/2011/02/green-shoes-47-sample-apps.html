Green Shoes 47 sample apps

February 4 2011, 10:08 PM  by ashbb

Have you installed green_shoes-0.138.0.gem ? :-D

Okay, let's play 47 sample apps from on built-in manual!

1. Store the following code:

    # green_shoes_manual.rb
    require 'green_shoes'
    Shoes.show_manual 'English'

2. run `ruby green_shoes_manula.rb`

Green Shoes manual will appear. You can click `sample` link at the side links.

Then click a thumbnail what you want to play with. ;-)

Have fun,
