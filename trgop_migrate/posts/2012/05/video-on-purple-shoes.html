Video on Purple Shoes

May 16 2012, 8:09 AM  by ashbb

If you install JMF (Java Media Framework API), you can embed MPEG-1 format video into Purple Shoes window.

Now is available MPEG-1 format only. But study more and find a solution to play other various popular formats, e.g. MP4, AVI, WMV and QuickTime.

Try out the latest Purple Shoes! :-D
