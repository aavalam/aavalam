Ruby Learning Core course sneak peak.

March 22 2012, 4:03 PM  by Victor Goff

When you first log in, after the start of the course, you will see information presented like this.

On the right hand side, you wiill see any courses you are participating in, as well as a link to our IRC Chat room, where you can interact with myself, and other students, past and present.

.

This is a representation of the layout of the forums that you will find.

This is from the 3rd week, which is incidentally the one with the most individual exercises to complete, and yet only one challenge.

As Satish writes in his description of the course it is a go at your own pace course: "you can login and start with the lessons any day and time and post your queries in the forum under the relevant lessons."

 

There aren't any webcasts so learning is generally asynchronous.  But like I mentioned earlier, we do have an IRC Channel for getting together and it is a good place to get together to do some pair programming.

I just wanted to give a sneak peak of some of the things to look forward to in the course.  We have organized it per week, and we leave the course material up for 4 weeks from the last day of the 7 week course.

Have you taken the course or are you looking to join?
