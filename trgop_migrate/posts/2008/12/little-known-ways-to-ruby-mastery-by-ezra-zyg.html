Little Known Ways to Ruby Mastery by Ezra Zygmuntowicz

December 2 2008, 4:27 AM  by Victor Goff
Interview answer from Ezra Zygmuntowicz to a question posed by Yours Truly...

Victor Goff, USA>> How do you see the market for Ruby Programmers in the work place, and do you see it as primarily tied to Rails and Web related work? Do you see trends in administration or other work? What’s the future for Ruby?
Ezra>> I see a huge demand for Ruby programmers and not near enough supply of good developers. Of course folks are always hiring for Rails web developers but I also see lots of jobs for Ruby that are not web related. Ruby is being used more and more as enterprise glue language and I think it really shines in that space.
