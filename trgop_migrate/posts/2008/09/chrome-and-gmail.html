Hmm... Seems like it is pretty strange, but Google Chrome doesn't seem
to work well with Google Mail. I am trying to write this message to
the blog, and my cursor keeps getting bumped to the end of the message
window, each time someone on my chat list updates their status.

Very frustrating. It doesn't seem to happen constantly, but then the
status doesn't constantly change. Just enough that I didn't notice it
until today.

track. It plays well in my limited memory, and for the most part is
very nice and light. But especially on Google sites, I would think
it would at least play exceptionally well.

Fortunately, Google seems to have a pretty fast update cycle...
Release early, update often.
